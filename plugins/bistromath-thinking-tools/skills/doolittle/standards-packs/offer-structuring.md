## Author interview questions (for pack extraction)

1. From the lender's chair, what makes an offer's financing actually strong — and what do agents
   *believe* makes it strong that doesn't?
2. Walk through the last three deals you saw lost on offer structure. What question, asked
   earlier, would have saved each?
3. Which contingencies do agents waive for the wrong reasons, and whose interest did each waiver
   really serve?
4. What's the evidence hierarchy for a buyer's financing claim (pre-qual letter → underwritten
   approval), and what should a listing agent demand at each level?
5. What promise about closing timeline can an agent never make, and what's the verified version
   of the same reassurance?
