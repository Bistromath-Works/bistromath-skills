# Mortgage Documentation Requirements

## Universal Requirements (All Loan Types)

- Most recent 2 months of bank statements (all pages, all accounts)
- Most recent pay stubs (30 days)
- 2 years of W-2s or tax returns (if self-employed)
- Valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license or passport)
- Proof of homeowner's insurance (required at final resubmission)
- Purchase contract/accepted offer
- Proof of earnest money deposit

## Conventional Loans

### Additional Requirements
- Gift letter (if applicable)
- Verification of down payment funds
- HOA information (if applicable)
- Condo questionnaire (if applicable)

### Self-Employed Borrowers Add
- 2 years personal tax returns (all schedules)
- 2 years business tax returns (all schedules)
- Year-to-date P&L statement
- Business license

## FHA Loans

### Additional Requirements
- All Universal Requirements
- FHA case number assignment confirmation
- Property must meet FHA minimum property standards
- FHA appraisal with required inspections

### Property-Specific
- Well/septic inspection (if applicable)
- Termite inspection report
- Any repairs identified in appraisal must be completed

## VA Loans

### Additional Requirements
- Certificate of Eligibility (COE)
- DD-214 (if applicable)
- Current military orders (if active duty)
- VA appraisal with required inspections

### Property-Specific
- Well/septic inspection (if applicable)
- Termite inspection report
- Any repairs identified in appraisal must be completed
- NOV (Notice of Value) from VA

## USDA Loans

### Additional Requirements
- Property must be in USDA-eligible rural area
- Income verification (USDA has income limits)
- USDA eligibility certificate
- Well/septic inspection (common in rural areas)
- Termite inspection report

## Jumbo Loans

### Additional Requirements (Stricter Documentation)
- 2-6 months of bank statements (lender dependent)
- Larger reserves required (typically 6-12 months PITI)
- Full tax returns (2 years) often required
- Additional asset documentation
- Appraisal may require second review

## DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) Loans

### Investment Property Specific
- Current lease agreement (if occupied)
- Property income documentation (rent roll)
- Property insurance
- HOA docs (if applicable)
- Entity documentation (if held in LLC)

### No Personal Income Requirements
- No pay stubs required
- No W-2s required
- No tax returns required
- Loan qualification based on property cash flow

## Bank Statement Loans

### Self-Employed/Non-Traditional Income
- 12-24 months of business bank statements (all pages)
- Business license
- Valid government ID
- Proof of business operation (website, invoices, etc.)

### No Tax Returns Required
- Income calculated from bank deposits
- Higher interest rates typically
- Larger down payment may be required
