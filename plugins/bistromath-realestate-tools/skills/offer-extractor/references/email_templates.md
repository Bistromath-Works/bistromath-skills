# Email Templates and Formatting

## Subject Line Formula

**Purchase Transaction:**
`Your [Property Address] Home Purchase - Next Steps ([X] min read, ACTION REQUIRED)`

**Refinance Transaction:**
`Your Refinance Application - Next Steps ([X] min read, ACTION REQUIRED)`

**Read time estimation:**
- 1-2 sections: 2 min
- 3-4 sections: 3 min
- 5+ sections or complex: 4-5 min

## Email Structure Template

### 1. Opening (Personalized Greeting)

**Purchase - Congratulatory tone:**
```
Hi [Client Name],

Congratulations on your accepted offer for [Property Address]! We're excited to help you get to the closing table and into your new home.

Below you'll find everything you need to know about your loan, important dates, and the documents we'll need from you.
```

**Refinance - Professional tone:**
```
Hi [Client Name],

Thank you for choosing [YOUR TEAM NAME] for your refinance. We're committed to making this process as smooth and efficient as possible.

Below is your loan overview, timeline, and required documentation to get started.
```

### 2. Loan Parameters Section

Use clear formatting with headers and bullet points:

```
💰 Your Loan Overview:

• Purchase Price: $[amount]
• Down Payment: $[amount] ([%]%)
• Loan Amount: $[amount]
• Loan Type: [Conventional/FHA/VA/USDA/Jumbo/DSCR/Bank Statement]
• Estimated Monthly Payment: $[amount] (P&I)
• Interest Rate: [rate]% (estimated - subject to rate lock)

💵 What You'll Need at Closing:

• Down Payment: $[amount]
• Estimated Closing Costs: $[amount]
• Total Cash Needed: $[amount]

[If applicable]
⚠️ Special Conditions:
• [List any special conditions from the offer]
```

### 3. Timeline Section

**Purchase:**
```
📅 Your Important Dates:

• Closing Date: [Date]
• Rate Lock Available: [Date] (earliest you can lock your rate)
• Initial Documentation Due: [Date] (2 days after meeting Chris)

[If applicable - Contract Contingencies]
📋 Contract Deadlines (from your offer):
• [Condition] Deadline: [Date]
• [Condition] Deadline: [Date]
```

**Refinance:**
```
📅 Your Refinance Timeline:

• Documentation Deadline: [Date] (48 hours to submit all docs)
• Expected Closing: Within 30 days from complete file

The faster you get us your documents, the faster we can close your loan!
```

```

• [ ] Proof of earnest money deposit

• [ ] [Specific requirements]

[If special circumstances]
**Property-Specific Requirements:**
• [ ] [Well/septic inspection]
• [ ] [Termite inspection]
```

### 6. Next Steps Section

**Purchase:**
```
✅ Your Next Steps:

1. **Wait for Chris's call** - He'll contact you within 1 business day to schedule a meeting
2. **Gather your documents** - Start collecting items from the checklist above
3. **Submit initial docs** - Send to Chris within 2 days of your meeting
4. **Rate lock decision** - We'll discuss rate lock options when available ([Date])

```

**Refinance:**
```

1. **Wait for Chris's call** - He'll contact you within 1 business day
2. **Submit all documents within 48 hours** - Speed is key for refinances
3. **Review and approve** - We'll process quickly once we have everything
4. **Close your loan** - Typically within 30 days of complete file

Questions? Reach out to Chris at any time using the contact information above.
```

## Formatting Best Practices

- Use emojis sparingly for visual breaks (📅 💰 📄 ✅ ⚠️ 👤)
- Bold important dates and dollar amounts
- Use bullet points for lists
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
- Use whitespace to separate sections
- Highlight hard deadlines with ⚠️
- Use checkboxes [ ] for action items

## Tone Guidelines

**Purchase transactions:**
- Congratulatory opening
- Friendly but professional
- Firm on deadlines ("no exceptions")
- Encouraging throughout

**Refinance transactions:**
- Professional and efficient
- Emphasize speed and responsiveness
- Less emotional, more transactional
- Focus on benefits (lower payment, cash out, etc.)

## Privacy Reminders

**NEVER include in emails:**
- Social security numbers
- Dates of birth
- Credit scores
- Credit report details
- Account balances (except in general loan overview)

If user asks to include these, confirm they understand the privacy risk.
