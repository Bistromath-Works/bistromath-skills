# Timeline Calculation Rules

## Purchase Transactions

All dates calculated working **backward** from the closing date.

### Key Milestones

1. **Closing Date**
   - Final date from accepted offer
   - All funds and documents must be complete

2. **Cleared to Close (CTC) Date**
   - Formula: Closing Date - 10 business days
   - Underwriting must be complete
   - All conditions satisfied

3. **Final Resubmission Date**
   - Formula: CTC Date - 5 calendar days
   - ALL documentation must be submitted
   - Homeowner's insurance policy required
   - No exceptions after this date

4. **Initial Resubmission Date**
   - Formula: 3 calendar days from Chris meeting
   - First batch of documentation due
   - Submitted 2 days after meeting with Chris

5. **Rate Lock Availability**
   - Formula: 30 calendar days prior to closing
   - Exclude weekends and federal holidays
   - Always round forward to next business day
   - Must be active business day to lock rate

### Business Day Calculation Rules

**Count as business days:**
- Monday through Friday
- Exclude federal holidays

**Federal Holidays to Exclude:**
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day (3rd Monday in January)
- Presidents' Day (3rd Monday in February)
- Memorial Day (Last Monday in May)
- Juneteenth (June 19)
- Independence Day (July 4)
- Labor Day (1st Monday in September)
- Columbus Day (2nd Monday in October)
- Veterans Day (November 11)
- Thanksgiving (4th Thursday in November)
- Christmas Day (December 25)

**Holiday observance rules:**
- If holiday falls on Saturday, observed on Friday
- If holiday falls on Sunday, observed on Monday

### Timeline Presentation Format

Present timelines in this order:
1. Closing Date (from offer)
2. Rate Lock Available Date
3. Initial Resubmission Date
4. Final Resubmission Date (ALL DOCS DUE)
5. Cleared to Close Date

**Example format:**
```
📅 Your Important Dates:

• Closing Date: [Date]
• Rate Lock Available: [Date] (earliest you can lock your rate)
• Initial Documentation Due: [Date] (2 days after meeting Chris)
• Final Documentation Due: [Date] ⚠️ HARD DEADLINE - ALL DOCUMENTS
• Cleared to Close: [Date] (loan fully approved)
```

## Refinance Transactions

### Standard Timeline
- **Total time to close:** 30 days maximum
- **Documentation deadline:** 48 hours after speaking with Chris
- **No rate lock calculations needed** (refinance can close anytime)

### Timeline Presentation Format

Present timeline in this order:
1. Today's date
2. Documentation deadline (48 hours from Chris contact)
3. Expected closing timeframe (30 days or less)

**Example format:**
```
📅 Your Refinance Timeline:

• Today: [Date]
• Documentation Deadline: [Date] (48 hours to submit all docs)
• Expected Closing: Within 30 days from complete file
```

## Special Considerations

### Urgent Closings
- If closing is less than 30 days away, flag as urgent
- Compress timeline and emphasize immediate action needed
- May not have time for rate lock window

### Extended Timelines
- If closing is more than 60 days away, note rate lock limitations
- Standard rate locks are 30-60 days
- May need extended rate lock (additional cost)

### Contract Contingencies
- Extract condition removal dates from offer
- These are separate from loan processing dates
- Include in timeline but clearly label as "contract contingencies"
- Common contingencies: inspection, appraisal, financing
